About Rajan Carpenter & Interior Works

Established in 2011, Rajan Carpenter & Interior Works has been the trusted name for premium carpentry and interior solutions in Borivali, Mumbai. Founded by Master Carpenter Mr. Rajan Verma, we started as a small workshop with a vision to deliver exceptional craftsmanship that combines traditional woodworking techniques with modern design sensibilities.

Today, our team of 15 skilled artisans serves over 5000 satisfied customers across Mumbai, specializing in custom furniture, door installations, and complete interior solutions. We take pride in our 25+ years of experience and our commitment to quality, precision, and customer satisfaction.
